What We Offer

Counselling

Our personalized counseling sessions are designed to support you through emotional and mental well-being challenges.

Awareness Events & Campaigns

We organize regular events and campaigns to raise mental health awareness and reduce stigma in the community.

Breathing Control Exercises

Practice breathing exercises with our in-built timer to reduce stress and anxiety, and improve focus.

Yoga

We organize regular yoga events and online meetings with yoga instructors for reducing stress and improving mental health.

Online Journaling

We provide our users with an online journaling facility. Journaling helps users to write their thoughts and feelings in a private space.

Goal Setting and Progress Tracking

We provide our users with a progress tracker. It allows users to set mental health goals and track their progress.
